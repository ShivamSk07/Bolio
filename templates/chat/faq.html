Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Bolio.

What is Bolio?

Bolio is a secure, real-time communication platform designed for privacy and professional use, featuring HD video calls, encrypted messaging, and time capsules.

Are my calls secure?

Yes, all video and voice calls use WebRTC with peer-to-peer encryption, ensuring that only the participants can access the media stream.

How do I report a bug?

You can use the "Report a Problem" link in settings to send us details about any issues you encounter.
