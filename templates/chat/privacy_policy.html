Privacy Policy

Your data security is our top priority.

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data required for communication, including your username, phone number, and profile information. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

2. Communication Security

Messages and calls are processed through encrypted channels. While we store messages to sync across devices, they are stored using industry-standard protection.

3. Private Accounts

If you enable a private account, only users you explicitly approve can initiate a conversation with you.

4. Media Storage

Media files (images, videos) provided in chats are stored securely. You can delete your data at any time through the "Delete Account" option in settings.
